IUCAA Centre for Astronomy Research and Development (ICARD)

have been established at department of Information technology Kannur university.

The primary functions of ICARD are:

Innovative Education

Enhancing astronomy education through modern courses and distance learning programs.

Workshops & Meetings

Regular workshops and meetings within universities and nearby regions.

Public Outreach

Active public outreach for astronomy education and awareness.

Visiting Centre

Serving as a visiting center for nearby astronomers.
